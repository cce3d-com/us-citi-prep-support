Getting Started

Welcome to Civics Cards! This streamlined app helps you prepare for your U.S. citizenship test with interactive flash cards and personalized settings.

Key Features:
  • Interactive flash cards with all 100 civics questions
  • Built-in shuffle functionality for varied practice
  • Audio pronunciation for all questions
  • State-specific personalization always available
  • Large, responsive cards optimized for all screen sizes

Flash Cards

Master all 100 civics questions with interactive flash cards designed for efficient studying on the go.

Using Flash Cards

  1. Tap a card to flip it and reveal the answer
  2. Swipe left or right to navigate between cards
  3. Tap the shuffle button (top right corner) to randomize card order
  4. Use the speaker button to hear audio pronunciation
  5. Cards automatically resize to fit your screen perfectly
Smart Features:
  • Built-in Shuffle - Shuffle button integrated into each card
  • Helpful Hints - Get tips on swiping and shuffling (shown first 3 times)
  • Audio Support - Clear pronunciation for all questions and answers
  • Responsive Design - Large cards that adapt to your device
  • All 100 Questions - Complete official USCIS civics test content
Pro Tip
Use the shuffle feature regularly to ensure you're not just memorizing the order. The hint system will guide you through the controls the first few times you use the app.

Settings

Customize your learning experience and personalize content for your state.

State Related Answers

Personalize your study experience with information specific to your state (always available in Civics Cards):

  • Governor - Set your current state governor
  • Capital - Set your state capital
  • U.S. Senator - Choose one of your state's senators
  • U.S. Representative - Select your representative
Once set, these personalized answers will appear in your flash cards, making your preparation more relevant and memorable. This feature is always available - no upgrade needed!

Audio Features

Take advantage of audio features to improve pronunciation and listening skills.

Audio Tip
During the actual citizenship test, questions will be read aloud. Practice listening to the audio versions to get comfortable with the pace and pronunciation.

Test Day Preparation

What to Expect

  • You'll be asked up to 10 civics questions
  • You must answer 6 correctly to pass
  • Questions cover all topics in this app
  • The officer will stop once you answer 6 correctly

Final Preparation Tips

  1. Study all 100 questions - Use the flash cards regularly
  2. Shuffle frequently - Don't just memorize the order
  3. Use state-specific answers - Set up your state information in Settings
  4. Practice with audio - Get used to hearing questions read aloud
  5. Stay calm and confident - You've prepared well!
Remember: This app contains all 100 official USCIS civics questions. Master these, and you'll be ready for your citizenship test!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many questions are on the actual test?

A: You'll be asked up to 10 questions, and you need to answer 6 correctly to pass.

Q: Are these the real test questions?

A: Yes! This app contains all 100 official USCIS civics questions that could appear on your test.

Q: Do I need to memorize all 100 questions?

A: Yes, you should study all questions since you won't know which 10 will be selected for your test.

Q: What if I don't know my representative?

A: Use the app's settings to look up and set your state's representatives. This information will be used in your study materials.

Q: Can I use this app offline?

A: Yes! All flash cards work without an internet connection.
