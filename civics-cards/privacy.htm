Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Introduction

Welcome to Civics Cards. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Summary: We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our app is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect or transmit personal information. All your study progress is stored locally on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what information we do NOT collect:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers
  • Study Progress: Your flash card progress and settings are stored only on your device
  • Location Data: We do not access or collect your location information
  • Contact Information: We do not access your contacts, calendar, or other personal data
  • Account Information: No account registration is required or collected
  • Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app or collect behavioral data

Information We Do Collect

App Settings

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • State Selection: Your chosen state for personalized civics questions
  • Representative Information: Governor, senators, and representative names you enter
  • Audio Preferences: Your audio and speech settings
  • Study Progress: Flash card usage and shuffle preferences

Device Information

Our app may access basic device functionality:

  • Audio System: To play question pronunciations and answers
  • Local Storage: To save your progress and settings
  • Display Settings: To optimize app appearance for your device

Important: All this information is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Advertising

Our app contains advertisements provided by Google AdMob to support free access to study materials. Google AdMob may collect and use information as described in their privacy policy:

  • Ad Personalization: Ads may be personalized based on your interests
  • Device Identifiers: Google may use device advertising IDs
  • Ad Performance: Information about ad interactions may be collected

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's advertising settings.

App Store

When you download our app from the App Store, Apple's privacy policy applies to that transaction and any App Store features you use.

How We Protect Your Information

Local Storage Only

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your information never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your study progress or settings
  • Your data is protected by your device's security features
  • Deleting the app removes all stored information

No Data Transmission

Our app works completely offline and does not require an internet connection for core functionality. This means:

  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • No risk of data breaches from our systems
  • Complete privacy and control over your information

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for individuals studying for U.S. citizenship, typically adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of the app and be aware that:

  • The app contains educational content about U.S. government and history
  • Advertisements may be displayed (as allowed by Google AdMob policies)
  • No personal information is collected or required

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app settings
  • Modification: You can change your state and representative information anytime
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
  • Portability: Your data remains on your device and is not transferable

Advertising Choices

You can control advertising preferences:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising
  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • Note that opting out may result in ads that are less relevant to your interests

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date below. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify users through:

  • App Store update notes
  • In-app notifications (if applicable)
  • Updates to this webpage

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@uscitizenshiptestapp.com

Subject Line: Privacy Policy Inquiry - Civics Cards

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within 30 days. Please include:

  • Your specific privacy concern or question
  • The version of the app you are using
  • Your device type and operating system version

Legal Compliance

California Residents (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have specific rights. However, since we do not collect personal information, these rights may not apply. If you have questions about CCPA compliance, please contact us.

European Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR principles. Since no personal data is collected or processed by our app, GDPR data subject rights related to personal data do not apply. Advertising partners may have separate obligations under GDPR.

Other Jurisdictions

We strive to comply with applicable privacy laws in all jurisdictions where our app is available. Our practice of not collecting personal information helps ensure broad compliance with privacy regulations.
